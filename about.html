About Me

Hi, I’m Alex Lee, a passionate Computer Science student and software developer based in Brooklyn, New York. My journey into computer science began in middle school when I joined my school's robotics club. That experience ignited a deep curiosity about coding and problem-solving, and from that point on, I’ve been hooked.

Over the years, I’ve grown to appreciate how technology can shape the world around us, and I’m driven by the idea of creating innovative solutions that improve people’s everyday lives. Whether it's through building intuitive software or solving complex challenges, I’m always eager to learn, collaborate, and make an impact.

I’m most proficient in Java and Python, but I’ve also worked with C, JavaScript, and HTML/CSS in past projects. I thrive in environments where creativity meets technical problem-solving, and I’m excited about contributing my skills to projects that make a real difference.
